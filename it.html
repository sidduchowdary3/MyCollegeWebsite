Welcome to the Department of Information Technology

Overview

The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 1998. The intake of the department is 120. The department got accreditation by the National Board of Accreditation in 2007 and reaccreditation in 2012. Once again, it is provisionally accredited for 3 years under OBE in the year 2016 and got the 4th time accreditation under OBE Tier 1 in 2019 for 3 academic years. The Department has 24 eminent faculty members, among them 11 with Ph.D. - Doctorate degrees, and 13 have M.Tech degrees in CSE, IT, IST, and Software Engineering. The department has 2 Professors, 3 Associate Professors, 2 Senior Assistant Professors, and 16 Assistant Professors.

The department is conducting the Infosys-campus connect program/Spring Board to train students to make them industry-ready. NPTEL online certification courses and IIT Bombay Spoken tutorials are initiated in the institute, and many from the IT department have been certified in various courses. AICTE has sanctioned an amount of ₹7.25 Lakhs to establish the Entrepreneurship Development Cell coordinated by Dr. J. Rajendra Prasad, former Head of IT. DST New Delhi sanctioned an amount of ₹6 Lakhs to Dr. B.V. Subba Rao, Professor, and Head of IT for the Big Data Initiative under BDI-Division of DST. Dr. D. Kavitha, Dr. Y. Suresh, and Dr. K. Pavan Kumar received minor research projects from UGC.

The Faculty of IT has published 17 patents and continues the filing process. All faculty members are enthusiastically involved in research activities. The faculty has published more than 150 research articles (out of which 30 articles are in Scopus/SCI) in various conferences, national and international journals. The Department is periodically organizing seminars, conferences, faculty development programs, workshops, and guest lectures for the benefit of both students and staff.

All passed-out students from the department have secured more than 75% placements. 2016 passed-out students got 66 placement offers, 2017 passed-out students got 99 placement offers, 2018 passed-out students got 116 placement offers, and 2019 passed-out students got 112 placement offers. The highest pay package offered was ₹7.60 Lakhs, with an average pay package of ₹4.01 Lakhs. Students are also actively participating in national-level competitions like SIH, AICTE-Chhatra Vishwakarma awards, and events conducted by NIRF rank institutions. Students are encouraged to participate in various professional society activities under CSI, ISTE chapters.

Students actively participate in APSSDC skill training programs and are also offered certification courses. They are also encouraged to take part in internships. In order to enhance creative abilities, the department has established an incubation center. Personality development programs are frequently conducted to enrich students' soft skills, helping them improve their commitment at all levels.

The department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing & ITES equipment worth ₹1.2 Crores. An internet bandwidth of 500 Mbps (1:1) leased line is available, supported by SOPHOS Firewall providing security. The department has well-established classrooms, e-classrooms, seminar halls, and laboratories equipped with LCD/LED projectors and good acoustics. The department has formed two quality circles focusing on skill enhancement through certifications and special attention to academically weak students to improve pass percentages.

The department has entered into MoUs with various academies like Red Hat, CISCO, AWS, Palo Alto, Blue Prism University, through which both faculty and students are certified in the latest trends and technologies. Faculty members are encouraged to involve in research activities through four research groups and are actively publishing papers in quality journals and applying for R&D projects and seed money.
